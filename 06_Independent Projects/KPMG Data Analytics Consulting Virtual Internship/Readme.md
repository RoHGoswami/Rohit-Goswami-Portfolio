# KPMG Data Analytics Virtual Internship: Sprocket Central Pty Ltd

## Project Overview
This repository contains the documentation and analytical outputs for the KPMG Data Analytics Virtual Internship. The project focuses on a data-driven approach to customer acquisition for Sprocket Central Pty Ltd, a retail organization specializing in bicycles and cycling accessories. The primary objective was to analyze existing customer datasets to identify trends and optimize marketing strategies for a prospective customer list of 1,000 individuals.

## Methodology and Task Documentation

### Phase 1: Data Quality Assessment
The initial phase involved a comprehensive audit of three primary datasets: Customer Demographics, Customer Address, and Transactions. The assessment was conducted against the following data quality dimensions:
* **Accuracy:** Identification of outliers and incorrect data entries.
* **Completeness:** Analysis of missing records and null values.
* **Consistency:** Evaluation of formatting discrepancies across variables.
* **Validity:** Verification of data against established business rules.

The findings were formalized in a technical memorandum outlining specific data integrity issues and proposed remediation strategies.

### Phase 2: Data Insights and Customer Segmentation
Following data preparation, a quantitative analysis was performed on the existing customer base to identify high-value segments. 
* **RFM Analysis:** Customers were categorized based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value to determine their lifetime value to the organization.
* **Trend Analysis:** Statistical correlations were identified between customer demographics (age, wealth segment, and industry) and purchase behavior.
* **Targeting:** A predictive approach was utilized to select the most profitable 1,000 new customers from the provided prospect list.

### Phase 3: Data Visualization and Executive Reporting
The final phase focused on the synthesis of analytical findings into a visual format suitable for executive stakeholders.
* **Dashboard Development:** Developed interactive visualizations to display demographic distributions and profit trends.
* **Strategic Recommendations:** Translated analytical insights into three core business strategies focused on optimizing marketing spend and increasing conversion rates within high-yield demographics.

## Technical Stack
* **Data Processing:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced Functions, Pivot Tables, Data Cleaning)
* **Data Visualization:** Tableau / Power BI
* **Communication:** Technical Writing and Executive Presentations
